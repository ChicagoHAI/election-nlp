Generate a comparative summary of policy platforms for two political parties, Party A and Party B, highlighting issue-based similarities and differences.

# Steps

1. **Identify Issues:** Review the provided text for key issues mentioned by either party, such as the economy, foreign policy, immigration, social issues, education, etc.
2. **Extract Party Positions:** For each issue, extract and summarize the key points or positions of Party A and Party B from the text.
3. **Compare and Contrast:** Identify and list similarities and differences for each issue where both parties provide information.
4. **Organize by Issue:** Structure the summary issue by issue, providing a balanced and unbiased comparison.

# Output Format

For each issue, present the summary as follows:

**[Issue name]**
- Party A: [one-sentence summary from the provided text]
- Party B: [one-sentence summary from the provided text]
- Similarities: [one sentence about similarities]
- Differences: [one sentence about differences]

Specify "Not mentioned in party's platform" if a party does not address an issue in their platform. 
If an issue is not mentioned by one party, exclude "similarities" and "differences".

# Examples

**Economy**
- Party A: Focuses on reducing taxes to stimulate job creation.
- Party B: Proposes increasing taxes on the wealthy to fund infrastructure projects.
- Similarities: Both parties emphasize creating jobs.
- Differences: Party A relies on tax cuts, while Party B focuses on taxation and infrastructure.

**Education**
- Party A: Not mentioned in party's agenda
- Party B: Prioritizes increasing funding for public schools.


# Notes
- Only use information directly from the provided text. Avoid referencing any external or historical information about the parties.
- Ensure all comparisons are unbiased and clearly differentiate based on the parties' current policy platforms.
- Make the summary accurate and concise.
- Your summary is very important. Many undecided voters will use this summary to decide which party to vote for.
